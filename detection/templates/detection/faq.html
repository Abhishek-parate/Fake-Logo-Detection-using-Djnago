Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Logo Shield

Logo Shield is an AI-powered logo authentication system that helps verify the authenticity of logos and brand assets. Our technology uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze logos and detect potential forgeries or manipulations, providing businesses and individuals with a reliable way to protect their brand identity.

Our AI system has achieved a 99.4% accuracy rate in detecting logo forgeries and manipulations. The system is continuously trained on millions of authentic and forged logos to ensure it stays ahead of even the most advanced forgery techniques. While no system is 100% perfect, Logo Shield represents the current state-of-the-art in logo authentication technology.

Logo Shield currently supports the following image formats:

  • PNG
  • JPG/JPEG
  • WebP
  • SVG
  • GIF (static only)

We recommend using PNG or SVG formats for logo analysis as they typically maintain the highest quality and detail, which helps our system provide the most accurate results.

Logo Shield uses a multi-layered approach to logo authentication:

  1. Pixel Analysis: Our system examines the pixel-level integrity of the logo to detect any inconsistencies or manipulations.
  2. Pattern Recognition: Using deep learning, we analyze patterns within the logo that are characteristic of authentic vs. generated images.
  3. Metadata Verification: We examine file metadata for indicators of editing or manipulation.
  4. Color Profile Analysis: Our AI checks for color consistency and gradients that may indicate forgery.
  5. Cross-Reference Verification: For registered brands, we can compare against our database of authentic logos.

These combined approaches allow our system to detect even sophisticated forgeries with high accuracy.
